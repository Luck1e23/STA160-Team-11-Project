Automated Disease Detection using Deep Learning

Thoracic diseases are a major global health concern, and chest x-rays are crucial for detecting thoracic abnormalities. As a non-invasive examination, chest x-rays give healthcare providers the necessary information to present a diagnosis and outline a treatment plan to address any abnormalities or concerning thoracic conditions. However, some findings can be missed when performing this examination. This can lead to errors in the interpretation of the x-ray and also hinder the care of the patient. Additionally, the rising cost of healthcare means that many individuals cannot afford second opinions in the case of missed findings, further compromising their care. We aim to create an accessible tool that identifies disease in x-rays using neural networks and machine learning. With this, chest abnormalities can be detected, supporting early diagnosis and improving the efficiency of modern healthcare while also reducing costs for individuals.
